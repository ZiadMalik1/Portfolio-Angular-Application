Telecommunications Between Quadcopters

Testing Speeds and Behaviors of Multiple Node Placements of Quadcopters trying to find ways to make the processes more effecient and reliable

More about the Assignment

Alongside Professor Jerry Miller, We tasked ourselves with creating extra parameters within the NS-3 Simulator for Drones. We wanted to add on more real-world variables that changed not only the behavior of the drones but also effected the telecommunication between them alongside the data that was being passed. We made this possible through Mathematical Calculations, Real-world Simulations, as well as testing through programming in C++ and Python. We are currently working on adding parameters such as Turn-radius, Wind-speed, and Turn-angle as parameters to the NS-3 Drone Simulator.

Working as a unit and a team has always been in my best interest as it allows for the development of better pieces for a greater whole
